Sheldon Cooper is one of the main characters of the hit tv show 'The Big Bang Theory'. He is a a 'child prodigy' and egotistical theoretical physicist that has the social skills of a rock. The show features many story lines that include him as the ever-condescending roommate of Leonard, who is a lowly experimental physicist. He enjoys hot beverages, trains and sitting in his spot.

He is one of the most popular regulars on the show. More about Sheldon Cooper can be found on Sheldon's Wikipedia page.
